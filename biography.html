    • Biography

    DJ Okawari is known for his unique approach to music, blending piano jazz and hip-hop influences to create a mellow yet soothing vibe. His song Luv Letter of the album Mirror was used in one of figure skater Daisuke Takahashi's routines at the 2010 World Figure Skating Championships. His music is also frequently played on YouTube; the videos have garnered views well above a million. The art for DJ Okawari's album covers were created by Japanese graphic artist Marumiyan.
