    • DJ Okawari

    Introduction

    DJ Okawari is an independent Japanese DJ, record producer and composer born and based in Shizuoka, Japan. His style mixes instrumental hip hop, hip hop, nu jazz, chillhop and japanese jazz. Piano is an instrumental theme in his music.In his career, he has released three studio albums: Diorama in 2008, Mirror in 2009, and Kaleidoscope in 2011.
